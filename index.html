About Oscillation

Oscillation 2K26 is the flagship annual techno-event organized by the Electronics & Computer Science (ECS) Department of VPPCOE & VA. The event brings together students from across the state to showcase their technical expertise, creativity, and competitive spirit through events like Code Debugging, Technical Quiz, PPT Presentation, Trivia, and an exciting BGMI tournament based on Battlegrounds Mobile India.Entirely student-driven, Oscillation 2K26 fosters innovation, teamwork, and professional growth in a dynamic and electrifying environment.
